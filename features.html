Features

Render

Render+ - Allows the user to control particles, animations, postfx, overlays, and hud elements.

StorageESP - Highlights storages through walls.

ItemESP - Highlights item entities on the ground.

DungeonESP - Highlights monster spawners underground. Useful for vanilla servers.

Fullbright - Brightens your game.

Breadcrumbs - Leaves a trail so you don't get lost.

Tracers - Draws a line towards entities.

Trajectories - Visualize the trajectory of a projectile.

XRay - You're superman!

CamZoom - Optifine Zoom.

HUD

DVDLogo - Renders a bouncing DVDLogo! Will it hit the corner?

Watermark - Adds a unique watermark.

PositionHud - Shows the player's coordinate position on the HUD.

ArmorHud - Displays the player's armor status.

ArrayList - Displays a list of active modules.

Speedometer - Visualizes your speed in KM/H.
